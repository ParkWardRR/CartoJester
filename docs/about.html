Understanding the Comedian Alliances Atlas

What is an "Alliance"?

An alliance represents a meaningful connection between two comedians. We track seven distinct types:

Collaboration

Co-starred in films, shows, or tours together

Troupe

Members of the same comedy group, cast, or ensemble

Influence

Documented comedic influence on another performer's style

Mentor

Direct mentorship or professional guidance relationship

Rivalry

Professional competition or creative rivalry between performers

Studio

Connected through the same studio, production company, or label

Family

Blood relatives who performed together or in the same field

Time Filtering

Every node and edge has optional time bounds. When you set a year range:

  • Nodes appear if their active period overlaps the filter range
  • Edges appear if their relationship period overlaps the range
  • Use the Play button to animate through comedy history
  • Quick-jump buttons let you focus on specific decades

Data Sources

The seed dataset is hand-curated with evidence links (primarily Wikipedia). Auto-ingested data comes from Wikidata queries processed through Apple ML deduplication on-device. All auto-ingested entries are flagged for verification.
